Scooped

Guess the word before you get Scooped!
Instructions
One Player Mode:
 You have 7 chances to guess the word, and a clue is provided to you. You lose a topping for every incorrect guess. If you guess the word before you lose your ice cream, you win!
Two Player Mode:
 Player 1 chooses a word for Player 2 to guess, and provides a clue.
Player 2 has 7 chances to guess the word, losing a topping for every incorrect guess.
Player 1 wins if Player 2 loses all their ice cream.
Player 2 wins if they guess the word right before they use up their 7 chances.

Chances: 7
