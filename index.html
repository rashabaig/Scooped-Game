Guess the word before you get Scooped

Instructions
Player 1 chooses a word for Player 2 to guess, and provides a clue.
Player 2 has 7 chances to guess the word, losing a topping for every incorrect guess.
Player 1 wins if Player 2 loses all their icecream.
Player 2 wins if they guess the word right before they use up their 7 chances.
